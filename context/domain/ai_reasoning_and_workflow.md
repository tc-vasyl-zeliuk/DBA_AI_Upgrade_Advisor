# AI Reasoning And Workflow

## AI responsibilities
- explain why an asset is urgent
- summarize blockers and readiness gaps
- draft per-asset upgrade briefs
- generate leadership summaries
- draft workflow payloads such as ticket or change request text

## AI restrictions
- do not create lifecycle truth
- do not assign severity without deterministic inputs
- do not recommend unsupported compatibility claims without evidence
- do not trigger production changes by default

## Workflow targets
- report outputs
- collaboration summaries
- ticket drafts
- change record drafts
- non-production rehearsal proposals

## Trust controls
- deterministic assessment first
- AI narrative second
- evidence attached
- confidence attached
- human approval for production execution
