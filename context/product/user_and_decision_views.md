# User And Decision Views

## Engineer and DBA view
Must answer:
- what is wrong
- why it matters
- how urgent it is
- what target version should be considered
- what blockers or prechecks exist
- what evidence supports the recommendation

## Manager and leadership view
Must answer:
- what is the current portfolio risk
- which teams own the riskiest assets
- where Extended Support cost is accumulating
- what should be completed in the next 30, 60, 90, and 180 days

## Design bias
Favor prioritization and evidence over raw telemetry density.
