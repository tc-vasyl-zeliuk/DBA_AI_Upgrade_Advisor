# Value Proposition

## Core value propositions

### 1. Service inventory and upgrade policy engine
Turn fragmented AWS service/version state into normalized assets evaluated against deterministic lifecycle and policy rules.

### 2. Support lifecycle knowledge base
Maintain refreshable support facts such as standard support end, Extended Support exposure, supported upgrade targets, and forced transition dates.

### 3. Upgrade impact model with AI reasoning
Combine deterministic scoring with AI-generated explanation so the product can rank assets, explain urgency, and draft evidence-backed preparation guidance.

### 4. Workflow layer
Convert assessments into operational outputs such as briefs, backlog candidates, alerts, ticket drafts, and leadership summaries.

## Why this product is strong
- It ties directly to operational risk.
- It exposes avoidable support cost.
- It supports earlier planning.
- It creates measurable outcomes instead of generic visibility.

## Outcome bias
This product should reduce emergency upgrade work, reduce avoidable support spend, and improve portfolio-level decision quality.
