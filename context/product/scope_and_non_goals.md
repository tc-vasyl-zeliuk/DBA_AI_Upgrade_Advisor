# Scope And Non-Goals

## MVP scope
- fleet inventory collection
- support lifecycle mapping
- risk and priority scoring
- per-asset upgrade brief generation
- portfolio reporting

## Out of scope for MVP
- autonomous production upgrades
- broad observability platform features
- generic chatbot behavior over AWS inventory
- multi-cloud governance from day one
- schema governance or data catalog expansion

## Decision boundary
The product is an upgrade governance and decision-support system, not a replacement for change management or production execution controls.
